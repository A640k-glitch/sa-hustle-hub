# Findings

## South African Side-Hustles (2026 Core List)

### 1. Digital Platforms (Zero Capital)
- **SagaPoll**: Surveys specifically for the African context. Pay in cash/airtime.
- **Respondent.io / Prolific**: High-paying international surveys (USD/GBP).
- **Toloka**: Data labeling for AI training.
- **RecruitMyMom**: Remote skilled work tailored for SA timezones.

### 2. Gig Economy & Local Services
- **Checkers Sixty60 / Uber Eats**: Delivery services (requires transport).
- **SweepSouth**: Domestic services.
- **Kandua**: Manual/Trade skills platform.

### 3. Education & Specialized
- **Superprof / Teach Me 2**: Online/local tutoring.
- **Cambly**: Conversational English tutoring.

## Constraints & Requirements
- **Data-Lite**: Users have limited mobile data (~100KB/page goal).
- **Verification**: SARS compliance monitoring is increasing; need to mention this in the "Legal/Tax" section.
- **Platform**: GitHub Pages is the chosen free hosting.
